🧠 What Is EAR & Blink Rate? (People WILL ask)
EAR (Eye Aspect Ratio)

“EAR measures how open the eye is.”

High EAR → Eye open

Low EAR → Eye closed or fatigued

Blink Rate

“Blink rate reduces when a person is mentally overloaded.”
